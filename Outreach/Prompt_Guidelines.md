# 🔥 Core Content Prompt (Use This Every Time)

```
Act as a senior Test Automation Architect and founder of an AI-driven QA company.

Context:
- I help startups and SaaS companies ship faster using AI-powered test automation.
- My expertise includes Playwright, Python, JavaScript, API automation, CI/CD, and building scalable automation frameworks.
- My audience is: startup founders, CTOs, and engineering leaders struggling with slow releases, flaky tests, and production bugs.
- My tone is: practical, slightly opinionated, experience-driven, and no fluff.

Task:
Write a LinkedIn post on the topic: "<INSERT TOPIC>"

Guidelines:
- Start with a strong hook (contrarian, insight, or pain point)
- Use short paragraphs (1–2 lines max)
- Write like a real engineer-founder, not a marketer
- Avoid generic statements and buzzwords
- Include a real-world perspective, lesson, or pattern
- Show authority through specificity (examples, systems, mistakes, observations)
- Keep it conversational and natural (no emojis overload, max 1–2 if needed)
- End with a subtle CTA that encourages discussion (NOT selling)

Structure:
1. Hook (scroll-stopping)
2. Problem or observation
3. Insight or lesson
4. Practical takeaway (what teams should do)
5. Closing line or question (to drive engagement) also ask to follow me @VaibhaV Arde for Agentic AI QA solutions

Output:
- Clean LinkedIn-ready post
- No hashtags OR max 5 relevant hashtags
- No AI-like phrases like - or ** or -- 
- Keep structure logic based paragraphs lets have approx 10 
- Keep minimum line spacing
```
